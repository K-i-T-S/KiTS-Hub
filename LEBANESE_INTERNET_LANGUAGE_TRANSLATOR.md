# Lebanese Internet Language (Arabizi) Translator

## Introduction

Lebanese Internet Language, commonly known as "Arabizi" (Arabic + Inglizi/English), is a modern dialect used primarily in online communication, social media, and text messaging. It combines English letters, numbers, and some Arabic characters to represent Lebanese Arabic words and phrases. This system emerged due to the limitations of early technology and keyboards that didn't support Arabic script, and has evolved into a cultural phenomenon among younger generations in Lebanon and across the Arab world.
