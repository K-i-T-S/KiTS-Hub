## ðŸ› ï¸ Technical Support

### System Requirements
**Minimum Requirements**:
- Modern web browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+)
- Stable internet connection (1 Mbps+)
- JavaScript enabled
- Cookies enabled

**Recommended Requirements**:
- Latest browser version
- High-speed internet (10 Mbps+)
- 1920x1080 resolution or higher
- 8GB+ RAM for optimal performance

### Common Technical Issues & Solutions

#### Login Issues
**Problem**: Cannot log in to account
**Solutions**:
1. Verify correct email and password
2. Check caps lock is off
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try password reset option
5. Ensure account is active (check email for verification)
6. Contact support if issue persists

#### Performance Issues
**Problem**: Slow loading or lag
**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection speed
2. Clear browser cache
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Try different browser
5. Check system resources
6. Ensure browser is updated

#### Data Sync Issues
**Problem**: Data not syncing between devices
**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify account is logged in on all devices
3. Manual sync refresh (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
4. Check for app updates
5. Contact support if issue continues

### Browser Compatibility
**Fully Supported**:
- Chrome (latest 2 versions)
- Firefox (latest 2 versions)
- Safari (latest 2 versions)
- Edge (latest 2 versions)

**Limited Support**:
- Internet Explorer (not recommended)
- Mobile browsers (use dedicated mobile apps)

## ðŸ’³ Billing & Account Management

### Subscription Management
**Upgrading Plans**:
1. Log in to account dashboard
2. Go to Settings > Billing
3. Select "Change Plan"
4. Choose new plan tier
5. Update payment information if needed
6. Confirm changes

**Downgrading Plans**:
1. Note: Downgrading takes effect at next billing cycle
2. Follow same steps as upgrading
3. Review feature changes before confirming
4. Export any data that may be lost

**Cancellation**:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Select "Cancel Subscription"
3. Choose cancellation reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Access continues until end of billing period

### Payment Methods
**Accepted Payment Methods**:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Bank Transfer (Enterprise accounts only)
- Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum - Enterprise only)

**Billing Cycles**:
- Monthly: Billed on same day each month
- Annual: 20% discount, billed once per year
- Custom: Enterprise arrangements

### Invoicing & Receipts
**Accessing Invoices**:
1. Log in to account
2. Go to Settings > Billing
3. Click "View Invoices"
4. Download PDF or email copies

**Tax Information**:
- VAT numbers accepted for EU customers

- Consult local tax advisor for specific requirements

## ðŸ“‹ Common Customer Questions

### General Questions
**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption, regular security audits, and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can export all your data at any time in various formats (CSV, PDF, Excel).

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers. After that, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

### Technical Questions
**Q: Do you have mobile apps?**
A: Yes, we have native iOS and Android apps for all major products. Download from App Store or Google Play.

**Q: Can I integrate with other software?**
A: Yes, we offer 100+ native integrations and a comprehensive API for custom integrations.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limits?**
A: You'll receive notifications at 80%, 90%, and 100% usage. You can upgrade anytime or purchase add-ons for additional capacity.

### Billing Questions
**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise accounts.

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
A: Yes, we offer 20% discount for annual billing and special pricing for nonprofits and educational institutions.
